✅ No active retirements or breaking changes in External ID right now. The tracker will surface them here as soon as Microsoft announces any.

How it works: tick the features that apply to your tenant, then click Save profile & filter. The tracker will instantly highlight every change relevant to your setup with a 🎯 Affects your tenant badge, and a new filter button lets you view only those changes.

🔒 Privacy: your selections are saved exclusively in your browser's localStorage — the same mechanism websites use to remember your preferences. No data is transmitted to any server, collected, stored, or shared. There is no account, no tracking, and no analytics tied to your choices. You can verify this yourself: open browser DevTools → Application → Local Storage → entratracker.aboutcloud.io and you will see your profile stored only there.

⚡ Stored in your browser only · No account needed · Reset anytime · Anonymous feature counts (e.g. "hybrid: yes") sent to our self-hosted Umami analytics — no personal data, no tenant names

ℹ️  How to read this tracker ▼
🔴 Red — Action required
Something is retiring or a breaking change is imminent. Check the deadline and act before it stops working silently.
🟡 Yellow — Review recommended
A change may affect your environment. No immediate break but worth reviewing your configuration.
🟢 Green — Informational
New capability or role available. No action required unless you want to adopt it.
⚫ Expired
Deadline has passed. Shown for reference — hidden by default, use the toggle to display.
RETIRING
Feature or API being removed from Microsoft Entra ID or Azure. Migrate before the deadline shown.
BREAKING
Configuration or script may break. Action required before the date shown — test in report-only mode first.
PREVIEW
Feature in public or private preview. Not recommended for production without thorough testing and a rollback plan.
NEW
New feature or capability released to general availability. Informational — evaluate for adoption.
